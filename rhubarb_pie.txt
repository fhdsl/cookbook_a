Grandma’s Rhubarb Custard Pie

Ingredients:
3 eggs
2 tablespoons milk
2 cups sugar
4 tablespoons flour
¾ teaspoon nutmeg
4 cups cut-up rhubarb
1 tablespoon butter
Pie crust

Instructions:
Beat 3 eggs slightly
Add 2 T. milk
Mix together and stir in 2 C. sugar, 4 T. flour, ¾ tsp nutmeg
Mix in 4 C. cut-up rhubarb
Dot filling in pan with 1 T. butter
Cover with lattice top (optional)
Bake 400 degrees for 50-60 minutes
